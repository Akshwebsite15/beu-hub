About BEU Hub

Mission

To put every resource a Bihar Engineering University student needs — papers, results, notices, notes and tools — in one fast, free, ad-light place, with zero sign-up friction.

Vision

A future where no BEU student is stuck searching ten different Telegram channels for one PDF.

Why GenZ BEU Hub

Built mobile-first, dark-mode by default, and designed the way students actually browse — quick cards, quick answers, no clutter.

Developer

Independently built and maintained by a small student-led team.

Privacy Policy

BEU Hub does not require sign-up, login, or email verification. We do not operate user accounts.

Attendance records, timetables, CGPA history and AI chat history are stored only in your browser's local storage, on your device. They are never uploaded to any server operated by BEU Hub.

Feedback submitted through the Reviews & Suggestions form (name/email optional) is stored locally on your device in this build; in a production deployment such feedback would be sent to the admin team for the sole purpose of improving the platform.

Links to third-party sites (BEU, government portals, educational platforms) are governed by their own privacy policies.
